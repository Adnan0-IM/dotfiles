# walls

A curated collection of high-quality wallpapers for various desktop environments and styles.

## Contents
This directory contains various image files, including:
- **macOS Styles**: Wallpapers from different macOS versions like Sonoma, Tahoe, and Mojave.
- **Windows Styles**: Iconic backgrounds from Windows 7, 10, and 11.
- **Linux & Desktop Environments**: Custom wallpapers for Ubuntu, Hyprland, and KDE.
- **Nature & Scenery**: High-resolution landscapes featuring mountains, lakes, oceans, and horizons.
- **Abstract & Digital Art**: Artistic compositions, including the "Aretha" series and works by Dominik Mayer.
- **Minimalist & Architectural**: Clean designs such as concrete buildings and simple gradients.
- **Pop Culture**: Themed wallpapers from series like *Frieren: Beyond Journey's End*.
- **Assorted**: A variety of other themes including space (Voyager), urban cityscapes, and tech-inspired backgrounds.
